Deepfake Detection

I'm working on a deepfake detection system with classmate Ahmed Hussain. Whereas most existing systems work by detecting subtle flaws in the fake media, we take the assumption that deepfake generation techniques will someday produce results that are truly indistinguishable from reality, so a different approach entirely is necessary. Our approach is to create and maintain a list of "valid" media, and all other media is considered "invalid." Since identifying all valid media is a daunting task, we start with the comparatively simple goal of recognizing and protecting news-related media.

We hash the media to store it in a space-efficient manner. We have largely reduced the overall problem to the fundamental technical problem of constructing a hash function that combines elements of cryptographic hashing and perceptual hashing. More information is available in the summary below.

The project has been recognized with a competition award, a national news interview, and conversations with a representative of the federal government and the Chief Data Scientist at Intel.
